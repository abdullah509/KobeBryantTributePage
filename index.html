Kobe Bryant

One of the greatest ever basketball players.

This is a timeline dedicated to Kobe Bryant:

  • 1996: Kobe was traded to the LA Lakers from the Hornets
  • 1997: Kobe Bryant was crowned the rookie slam dunk champion at the age of 18
  • 1998: At 19, Kobe became the youngest starter ever in his first NBA All-Star Game.
  • 1999-2000: Kobe Bryant wins his first NBA title as the Lakers beat the Indiana Pacers in six games.
  • 2000-01: Bryant averages 28.5 points for the season as the Lakers storm to their second straight NBA title
  • 2001-02: The Lakers win their third consecutive NBA title, sweeping the New Jersey Nets 4-0 in the Finals.
  • 2002: Kobe was named All-Star Game MVP for the first time. He also won the honor in 2007, 2009 and 2011.
  • 2004: Bryant and O’Neal had butted heads since they first teamed up in 1996.
  • 2006: On Jan. 22 Bryant scored a career-high 81 points against the Toronto Raptors.
  • 2006: Kobe won gold at the olympics with the mens USA basketball team
  • 2009-10: Kobe Bryant won his 4th championship. And in 2010, the Lakers outlasted the Celtics to win Game 7, 83-79. It was Bryant’s fifth and final championship ring.
  • 2012: Kobe bryant won his last olympic gold medal in London
  • 2016: Kobe Bryant annouces his retirement
  • 2018: Kobe won an Oscar for Best Animated short Film at the 90th Academy Awards.
